# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This document provides role-specific onboarding checklists to help new team members quickly understand their responsibilities, key interactions, and essential resources for their role in OctoAcme projects.

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Complete OctoAcme orientation and company onboarding
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document
- [ ] Review [Responsibility Matrix](octoacme-responsibility-matrix.md) for your role
- [ ] Set up access to GitHub, project boards, and communication channels
- [ ] Meet with your manager to discuss role expectations
- [ ] Identify your backup/shadow for continuity

---

## Developer Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Set up development environment and tooling
- [ ] Clone relevant repositories and review README files
- [ ] Attend daily standup and introduce yourself
- [ ] Review coding standards and PR workflow documentation
- [ ] Shadow code review sessions
- [ ] Review current sprint backlog and upcoming work

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Pick up first starter ticket (clearly scoped, well-defined)
- [ ] Submit first pull request and participate in code review
- [ ] Attend sprint planning and retrospective meetings
- [ ] Review test coverage requirements and CI/CD pipelines
- [ ] Pair program with experienced team member
- [ ] Understand deployment and release processes

### Ongoing
- [ ] Participate in technical design discussions
- [ ] Contribute to documentation updates
- [ ] Provide input on risk identification and estimation
- [ ] Share feedback in retrospectives

---

## Product Manager Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Review product vision, roadmap, and key metrics
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders and understand their needs
- [ ] Review current backlog and prioritization framework
- [ ] Attend user research sessions or review recent findings
- [ ] Understand success metrics and how they're measured
- [ ] Review previous sprint demos and retrospectives

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Shadow sprint planning and backlog refinement sessions
- [ ] Begin drafting user stories and acceptance criteria
- [ ] Present at sprint demo or stakeholder update
- [ ] Review analytics dashboards and key product metrics
- [ ] Collaborate with Project Manager on timeline and scope
- [ ] Meet with customer success team to gather feedback

### Ongoing
- [ ] Lead sprint planning and backlog prioritization
- [ ] Conduct user research and validate solutions
- [ ] Define and track success metrics for features
- [ ] Participate in cross-functional alignment meetings
- [ ] Make trade-off decisions with engineering and stakeholders

---

## Project Manager Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Review active projects, timelines, and current status
- [ ] Meet with project teams and key stakeholders
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) guide
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision-making processes
- [ ] Access project boards, risk registers, and documentation
- [ ] Shadow existing PM in daily standups and status meetings

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Facilitate your first daily standup
- [ ] Update project status report and communicate to stakeholders
- [ ] Review and update risk register
- [ ] Participate in sprint planning and retrospective
- [ ] Create or update project documentation
- [ ] Begin managing dependencies and blockers

### Ongoing
- [ ] Own project plan and timeline management
- [ ] Lead stakeholder communication and status reporting
- [ ] Manage risk register and escalations
- [ ] Facilitate team ceremonies (planning, demos, retros)
- [ ] Coordinate cross-team dependencies

---

## Process Owner Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Review all current process documentation in docs/ folder
- [ ] Understand OctoAcme's process evolution history and rationale
- [ ] Meet with previous Process Owner for knowledge transfer
- [ ] Identify key process stakeholders across teams
- [ ] Review recent process improvement feedback and requests
- [ ] Access process documentation repositories and wikis

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Shadow process review sessions with teams
- [ ] Conduct listening tour to gather feedback on current processes
- [ ] Review process adoption metrics (if available)
- [ ] Identify quick wins for process improvements
- [ ] Establish regular process review cadence
- [ ] Create communication plan for process updates

### Ongoing
- [ ] Conduct quarterly process documentation reviews
- [ ] Gather and analyze team feedback on process effectiveness
- [ ] Propose and implement process improvements
- [ ] Maintain process documentation currency and accuracy
- [ ] Facilitate process training and adoption
- [ ] Track process improvement initiatives

---

## Stakeholder Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Understand project objectives and business context
- [ ] Review Project One-pager and success metrics
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and Product Manager
- [ ] Review communication plan and meeting cadence
- [ ] Understand decision-making authority and approval processes
- [ ] Review current project status and timeline

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Attend sprint demo or milestone review
- [ ] Provide feedback on deliverables
- [ ] Review risk register and understand key risks
- [ ] Clarify requirements and priorities with Product Manager
- [ ] Participate in major decision discussions
- [ ] Understand escalation paths for issues

### Ongoing
- [ ] Attend regular milestone reviews and demos
- [ ] Review and approve major project decisions
- [ ] Provide timely feedback on deliverables
- [ ] Escalate business-impacting issues
- [ ] Participate in retrospectives (as appropriate)
- [ ] Sign off on milestone completions

## Communication Lead Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Review existing communication channels and cadence
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand stakeholder landscape
- [ ] Access all communication tools and platforms
- [ ] Review communication templates and formats
- [ ] Understand stakeholder information needs and preferences
- [ ] Review recent status reports and stakeholder updates

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Shadow existing communication activities
- [ ] Begin coordinating team check-ins and standups
- [ ] Draft and send your first status update
- [ ] Meet with all stakeholder groups to understand needs
- [ ] Review communication effectiveness metrics (if available)
- [ ] Identify gaps or improvements in current communications

### Ongoing
- [ ] Coordinate daily standups and team check-ins
- [ ] Prepare and distribute regular status reports
- [ ] Manage stakeholder briefings and updates
- [ ] Handle escalation communications
- [ ] Track and improve communication effectiveness
- [ ] Maintain communication channels and templates

## Risk Manager Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Review [Risks & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) guide
- [ ] Access and review current risk register
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand project context
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and risk response processes
- [ ] Review historical risks and how they were resolved
- [ ] Identify key risk categories for your projects

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Shadow risk assessment sessions
- [ ] Update risk register with new risks or status changes
- [ ] Participate in sprint planning risk discussions
- [ ] Develop mitigation plans for identified risks
- [ ] Practice risk escalation procedures
- [ ] Meet with technical leads to identify technical risks

### Ongoing
- [ ] Facilitate regular risk assessment sessions
- [ ] Maintain and update risk register weekly
- [ ] Monitor risk indicators and early warning signals
- [ ] Develop and track mitigation plans
- [ ] Escalate high-impact risks promptly
- [ ] Report on risk trends and patterns
- [ ] Coordinate risk response actions across team

## Change Champion Onboarding

### Week 1
- [ ] Understand organizational change management approach
- [ ] Review current and recent change initiatives
- [ ] Meet with Process Owner to align on improvement areas
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders for change initiatives
- [ ] Review change adoption metrics and feedback mechanisms
- [ ] Understand organizational culture and readiness for change

### Week 2-4
- [ ] Shadow ongoing change management activities
- [ ] Participate in process improvement discussions
- [ ] Begin gathering feedback on potential improvements
- [ ] Develop change proposal or business case
- [ ] Plan training or communication for a change initiative
- [ ] Identify early adopters and change advocates

### Ongoing
- [ ] Identify opportunities for beneficial changes
- [ ] Develop and execute change management plans
- [ ] Provide training and support during transitions
- [ ] Monitor adoption metrics and gather feedback
- [ ] Address resistance and facilitate smooth transitions
- [ ] Celebrate successes and share lessons learned
- [ ] Partner with Process Owner on improvements

## Tips for Successful Onboarding

1. **Ask Questions**: Don't hesitate to reach out to team members for clarification
2. **Take Notes**: Document your learning and questions as you go
3. **Shadow Others**: Learn by observing experienced team members
4. **Start Small**: Begin with manageable tasks and gradually increase complexity
5. **Seek Feedback**: Regularly check in with your manager and team
6. **Connect with Peers**: Build relationships across roles and teams
7. **Review Documentation**: Regularly refer back to process docs and guides
8. **Share Feedback**: Help improve onboarding by sharing your experience
